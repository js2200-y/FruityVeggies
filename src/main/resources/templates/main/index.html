Discarded apple, beauty lost, nature's sorrow sings.

December 14, 2020 by Chris

For the "red apple" we take for granted, additional labor costs are incurred, and huge amounts of agricultural waste are generated every year. The good thing about apples like that!!

  • No matter the color and size, it didn't make any difference in taste
  • Apples that naturally blend into the scenery of mountains and fields will taste, smell, and be more natural!
  • These wounds, which are treated as "ugly" in the market, are rather a "decoration" that has endured a strong environment and become more delicious.

Even if we can't change a lot right away, we will do our best to minimize agricultural products that are discarded because of their appearance by continuously telling stories and tasting them.
